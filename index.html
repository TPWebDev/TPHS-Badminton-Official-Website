About Us

We're one of the newest CIF teams at Torrey Pines High School, formed in 2015. We play in the Northern League with other San Diego City Conference and North County Conference schools, and just recently we were declared the undefeated league champions in the 2021 season!

Schedule Update March 20, 2025
Stay Classy Tournament Update

The Stay Classy Tournament, a yearly singles tournament held at Mira Mesa High School, will now be split into two times due to space constraints.

Freshmen and sophomores play on March 28th and March 29th; juniors and seniors will play on April 18th and April 19th.
